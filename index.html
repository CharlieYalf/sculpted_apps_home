BySculptedApps

Independent App Holdings by Jordan (Charlie) Morris

BySculptedApps is a holding space for independently built software, created and maintained by Jordan (Charlie) Morris.

In January 2025, I started a new chapter—teaching myself iOS development with a focus on Swift and SwiftUI. Since then, I've been learning in public, launching projects that reflect both technical curiosity and a deep commitment to accessibility and user experience.

At the moment, many of the apps being launched are part of an iterative learning journey - small, focused experiments that help refine my skills while offering useful or playful experiences to others.

Every app under the BySculptedApps umbrella is designed with intention. Whether it’s a creative experiment, a learning aid, or a simple tool for productivity, each product is crafted to be intuitive, inclusive, and built to last.

This isn't a venture-backed factory of features. It's a bootstrapped, fast-paced, and fun way to learn and build.

If you're interested in collaborating, acquiring existing apps, or just want to chat, please reach out to me at charlie@bysculptedapps.com.

New launches are on the way.
